ABOUT US
GENERAL INFORMATION

We are Kirin Gaming, known in the past as Small Gaming. We are a starting eSport Club, Content Creator Supporters and Charity Event Founders and we aim to be the best in all those sectors.

GROWING ESPORTS CLUB

We started our eSports Club project within our rebranding to Kirin Gaming in January, 2022. In our presentation streaming we revealed two Rocket League rosters, the League of Legends roster and finally the VALORANT one. But we are willing to bring Kirin Gaming to many more eSport Games.

CONTENT CREATOR SUPPORT

With Small Gaming, our main focus was to help small streamers develop in Twitch's enviroment. Now that we are Kirin Gaming, we are going to still be helping many small streamers. Also, we started the Kirin Gaming Content Creators Squad with 20 different streamers of various games:

CHARITY EVENTS

We make charity events to help a wide variety of associations in order to make a better world. We are in contact with associations in many different scopes, each event benefit will be given to a different association.

LASTEST NEWS IN OUR SOCIALS

All our Socials are up to date with the lastest information of our community. Stay tunned to all of them to know more about your favourite organization. You can follow all them in the contact section.
